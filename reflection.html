Reflection

Since I was a teenager, I worked for my family business as a welder. My life path seemed pretty straightforward and I never thought I would be returning to college to further my education. I started this semester with a lot of changes happening in life.

Going back to school had me feeling nervous and anxious about the changes happening, while also having feelings of being in over my head. Doing a daily 5-minute website and putting together simple programs built my confidence little by little, without my realizing it. Before I knew it, the end of the semester had snuck up on me. Looking through previous projects and being able to see the progress is a real boost to my confidence and empowering for the future.

I am beginning down a new path in life by going back to school and purusing a degree in computer science. I have always had a facination with computers and how they worked. From playing games, to surfing the web, to tinkering with making my own game servers to play on and modify: these were fun hobbies I could sink myself into, but I never thought it could be more than a hobby. Being in a position to further my knowledge in these areas and the opportunities that will follow feels like the sunrise on a bright and warm future.
